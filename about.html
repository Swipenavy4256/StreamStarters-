About StreamStarters

Helping the next generation of creators find their voice online.

What Is StreamStarters?

StreamStarters is a free educational resource for kids and teens who want to learn how to start streaming on YouTube or Twitch. We break down everything — from setting up your account to growing your audience — in plain, easy-to-understand language.

Our goal is simple: give young creators the knowledge they need to get started, without the jargon, the expensive courses, or the confusing tech talk.

🎯 Our mission: Make streaming accessible, safe, and fun for every young creator — no matter their experience level or budget.

Who Is This For?

StreamStarters is made for kids and teens aged 10–17 who are curious about streaming but don't know where to start. Whether you want to stream gaming, art, music, or just chatting — we've got guides for you.

Parents are also welcome here! We have a full Safety Guide designed to help young streamers stay safe online, and we encourage parents to read through our content before their child starts streaming.

What You'll Find Here

Our guides cover how to start on YouTube and Twitch, what free gear and software to use, how to grow your stream from zero, and — most importantly — how to stay safe online as a young creator.

All of our content is free. We keep the lights on through display advertising.

A Note on Safety

We take online safety seriously. All of our guides include safety reminders, and we have a dedicated safety guide for young streamers. We strongly recommend that anyone under 13 stream with a parent or guardian's knowledge and supervision.
