⚡ 4 Steps to Your First Stream

It's easier than you think. Here's the basic roadmap.

1

Pick Your Platform

Choose YouTube or Twitch — both are free and great for beginners.

2

Set Up Your Account

Create your channel or profile. Pick a cool name and add a profile pic.

3

Get Your Software

Download free streaming software like OBS or Streamlabs — no cost at all.

4

Go Live!

Hit that "Go Live" button. Your first stream won't be perfect — that's okay!
