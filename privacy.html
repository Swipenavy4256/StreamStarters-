Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

StreamStarters ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website streamstarters.online. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our site. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all visitors, especially children and young teens.

Information We Collect

StreamStarters does not require users to create accounts or submit personal information to access our content. We do not knowingly collect personal information from visitors. Our site may use standard analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to collect anonymous data such as page views and general geographic region. This data is not linked to any individual.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

StreamStarters is intended for use by young people, including children under 13. In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), we do not knowingly collect, use, or disclose personal information from children under 13. We do not require registration, account creation, or any form submission to use this site. If you believe your child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us at hello@streamstarters.online and we will promptly delete it.

Advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. We do not have control over the ads served by Google, but we have configured our AdSense account to comply with Google's policies for sites directed at children.

Note: Google may serve non-personalized ads only to users it identifies as being under 13, in accordance with applicable laws.

Cookies

Our site may use cookies for analytics and advertising purposes as described above. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of third-party services.

Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites (such as Twitch, YouTube, OBS Studio, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. We encourage parents and guardians to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites their children visit.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: hello@streamstarters.online
